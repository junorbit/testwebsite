About Me

Hello! I'm a Miami-born 20 year old artist studying Game Design and the University of Central Florida. I was heavily inspired by the video games I grew up playing as a kid to start drawing and making up my own characters, from printer paper and crayons to digital tablet and pen.

I'm studying game art and digital media as an art form in hopes of pursuing character design, storyboarding, and concept art in the game industry in the future. I aim for bright and bold colors with interesting shape language in my art style.
